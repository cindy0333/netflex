    Our theatrical poster for Land of Mine out in August 4th

    Incredible World War II film, ‘Land of Mine’ is in Cinemas August 4. Print campaign by Coffee & Cigarette and Highly recommended: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GWnwiqIWI0I

    In the aftermath of World War II, a group of surrendered German soldiers are ordered by Allied forces to remove their own landmines from the coast of Denmark.

    Directed by Martin Zandvliet.
